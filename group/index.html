Information for prospective students

I typically advise one PhD student per year and several Master’s students per year. Students interested in working with me should apply directly to the PhD or Master’s programs in Statistics or Computational and Applied Mathematics, and feel free to reach out to me to discuss potential research directions or any other questions once you are accepted to your program. Unfortunately I am usually not able to respond to emails from prospective students who have not yet been accepted to our programs.
